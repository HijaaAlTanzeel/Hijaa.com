Our Teaching Method

Our Method:
Learning Arabic by relying on English (or any other language) will not make you speak Arabic fluently.
Never.
This method limits your abilities and keeps you in a translation-dependent mindset.
Just imagine: You start by thinking of your sentences in your native language, then translate them into Arabic in your mind.
Can you picture this process?
Do you think you can become bilingual or even speak Arabic fluently this way?
It's not natural for your brain, and this teaching method is limited.
Therefore, we have developed our  teaching method, which relies on Modern Standard Arabic (MSA).

What the FAQ?

Find answers to frequently asked questions. 

In what language do the courses take place?

The courses take place exclusively in literary Arabic.

Although the aim of the courses is not to learn the Arabic language, studying the sciences of the Quran in Arabic can only be beneficial for you.

In addition, our teachers are trained to teach Arabic speakers.

Do you offer a trial class?

No, one session is not enough to get an idea of the effectiveness of a course, especially for an immersive method.

You can commit to one month only and decide whether you want to continue later or not (you can cancel your monthly subscription before automatic renewal)

What happens if I am absent?

In order for your class to be caught up, you must inform us of your absence (via your student area) up to 2 hours before your class.

You are entitled to one absence per month and per schedule (example: if you study 1 time per week, you are entitled to 1 absence per month). If you study twice a week, you are entitled to 2 absences per month. And so on.)

Other absences will not be reported.

So be sure to choose a schedule that suits you before registering.

Do you offer discounts?

We offer a discounted rate for a semi-annual or annual commitment (see the price section).

How is the course schedule drawn up?

You choose your schedule yourself when registering.

When do classes start?

Classes start as soon as you register. The start date of the courses is specified in the confirmation email (and on the registration page).
